What We Are Building

A halal savings product for the 1.8 billion Muslims who have been excluded from DeFi by design. Yield from trading fees, not interest. Full transparency. Scholar oversight.

☶

Sharia-Compliant by Design

Yield is generated from trading fees earned through liquidity provision, not from lending or interest. The product is structured as a Wakala arrangement with independent Sharia scholar oversight.

⚖

Non-Custodial and Transparent

Users maintain full control of their assets. Every transaction is verifiable on-chain. No intermediaries, no hidden mechanisms, no trust assumptions beyond the smart contract itself.

★

Built for the Long Term

This is not a speculative token project. It is financial infrastructure for a $3.8 trillion market that has zero representation in decentralized finance. We are building slowly and correctly.
